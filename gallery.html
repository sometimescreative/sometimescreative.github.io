Liberia E-Book Cover

Once again I wanted to create something that reflected my personal interest in history, which led to the creation of this fictional e-book cover. Due to the old nature of the background photo used, I attempted to touch up the imperfections of the original photo. I also utilized the Liberian flag as a background element. Created using Adobe InDesign and Adobe Photoshop.

Fox Tutoring Services Logo

This was a logo I created for another fictional company, Fox Tutoring Services. Foxes are stereotypically known for being cunning and clever, so I figured it would make sense to use it for a logo relating to tutoring services. Initially the orange line underneath was a ruler, but the lines were an issue when the logo was made smaller, so I settled on a simple line instead. Created using Adobe Illustrator.
